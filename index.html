Water Modelling & Monitoring Hub

Project Description

The NSW Modelling and Monitoring Hub (MaMH) is a knowledge network led by a state-wide representative Working Group with agreed processes established to coordinate and aid information sharing across government agencies and state-owned corporations in relation to water modelling and monitoring.

Members

  • Department of Finance, Services & Innovation, Manly Hydraulics Laboratory
  • Department of Industry, Land and Water
  • Department of Planning & Environment, Office of Environment & Heritage and Office of Local Government
  • Sydney Water
  • WaterNSW
